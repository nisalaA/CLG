Pure Origins. Perfect Brilliance.


Nestled in the heart of Colombo, Ceylon Lotus Gems Pvt Ltd stands as a trusted name in Sri Lanka’s renowned gemstone industry. As a licensed gem exporter, we are dedicated to bringing the natural brilliance of gemstones to the global market with authenticity, artistry, and integrity.

Our legacy is inspired by the purity of the lotus — a symbol of beauty and enlightenment — and by Sri Lanka’s centuries-old gem heritage that has captivated the world since ancient times. Each gemstone we offer is a reflection of that legacy, carefully selected from ethical sources, precisely cut by master craftsmen, and certified for authenticity and quality.

At Ceylon Lotus Gems, we believe that every gemstone tells a story — a story of nature's artistry and human expertise working in harmony. From the velvety blues of Ceylon sapphires to the vibrant hues of spinels, and garnets, our collection celebrates the rare and radiant treasures of the island.

We take pride in maintaining the highest standards of trust, transparency, and sustainability. Our commitment extends beyond trade — it is a promise to preserve Sri Lanka’s gem heritage while building long-term relationships with clients across the world.

Whether you are a jeweler, collector, or connoisseur, Ceylon Lotus Gems Pvt Ltd offers you more than gemstones — we offer a piece of Sri Lanka’s timeless beauty, perfected for the world.

Our Vision


To be the world's most trusted and inspiring source for ethically sourced, exquisitely crafted gemstones, illuminating lives with timeless beauty and elegance. We envision a world where every gem from Ceylon Lotus Gems is a cherished symbol of artistry, integrity, and natural wonder.

Our Mission


Our mission is to provide access to the finest quality gems, crafted into timeless pieces that reflect the unique beauty of each stone. We strive to educate and inspire our customers, fostering a deeper appreciation for the artistry and natural wonders behind every gem, while upholding the highest standards of integrity and sustainability.

Company Values

Authenticity


Every gemstone is ethically sourced and certified genuine — a true reflection of Sri Lanka’s natural treasure.

Craftsmanship


Our skilled gem cutters and polishers transform rough stones into exquisite works of art, preserving traditional techniques.

Trust & Integrity


Transparency and honesty guide every transaction, from sourcing to export, ensuring confidence among global clients.

Sustainability


Committed to responsible mining and fair trade practices that protect the environment and support local communities.
